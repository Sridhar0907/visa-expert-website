Personal Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa appointment confirmation letter
  • Passport-style photographs (2x2 inches)
  • Previous passports (if any)

Employment Documents

  • Approved I-797 petition (original)
  • LCA (Labor Condition Application)
  • Job offer letter from US employer
  • Employment contract
  • Company registration documents
  • Employer's tax returns

Educational Qualifications

  • University degree certificates
  • Official transcripts
  • Credential evaluation (if degree from outside US)
  • Professional certifications

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Pay stubs from current employer
  • Tax returns (last 2-3 years)
  • Salary certificate
