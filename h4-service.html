Personal Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa appointment confirmation letter
  • Passport-style photographs (2x2 inches)
  • Birth certificate (for children)

Relationship Documents

  • Marriage certificate (for spouse)
  • Birth certificate showing relationship to H1B holder
  • Family photographs
  • Joint bank account statements
  • Joint property documents (if any)

H1B Holder's Documents

  • H1B holder's passport copy
  • H1B visa copy
  • I-797 approval notice
  • Employment letter
  • Recent pay stubs
  • Tax returns

Financial Support

  • Bank statements
  • Affidavit of support (I-864)
  • Sponsor's employment verification
  • Income tax returns
