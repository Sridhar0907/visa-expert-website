ðŸ“‹ Required Documents for F1 Visa

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa appointment confirmation letter
  • Passport-style photographs (2x2 inches)
  • SEVIS fee receipt (I-901)

Academic Documents

  • I-20 form from US school
  • Admission letter from university
  • Academic transcripts (all levels)
  • Degree certificates
  • Standardized test scores (TOEFL, IELTS, GRE, GMAT)
  • School brochure/catalog

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements (last 6-12 months)
  • Scholarship/assistantship letters
  • Sponsor's financial documents
  • Affidavit of support
  • Education loan approval letter
  • Fixed deposit certificates
  • Property valuation documents

Sponsor Documents (if applicable)

  • Sponsor's employment letter
  • Sponsor's tax returns
  • Sponsor's bank statements
  • Relationship proof with sponsor
  • Sponsor's passport copy

Ties to Home Country

  • Family photographs
  • Property documents
  • Employment letter (if working)
  • Future career plans statement
  • Social ties documentation
