How to Apply for an Indian Passport - Complete Guide

Step 1: Visit Passport Seva Portal

Go to https://passportindia.gov.in and register yourself as a new user, then login to your account.

Step 2: Start Application Process

  • Click the category 'Apply for Fresh Passport/Reissue of Passport'
  • Click the highlighted link to fill the application form online

Step 3: Fill Application Form Details

3.1 Basic Information Section

Required Fields:

  • Application Type: Fresh Passport/Re-issue/Renewal
  • Passport Booklet Type: 36 pages (‚Çπ1,500) or 60 pages (‚Çπ2,000)
  • Validity Required: 10 years (for 18+ applicants)
  • Reason for applying: First time/Lost/Damaged/Expired

3.2 Personal Details Section

Fill Exactly as per Documents:

  • Given Name (First Name): As per birth certificate
  • Surname (Last Name): Family name
  • Date of Birth: DD/MM/YYYY format
  • Place of Birth: City, State, Country
  • Gender: Male/Female/Transgender
  • Marital Status: Single/Married/Divorced/Widowed
  • Educational Qualification: Highest degree
  • Employment Type: Government/Private/Self-employed/Student

3.3 Family Details Section

Parent Information (Mandatory):

  • Father's Name: Full name as per documents
  • Father's Date of Birth: If known
  • Father's Place of Birth: City, State, Country
  • Mother's Name: Full maiden name
  • Mother's Date of Birth: If known
  • Mother's Place of Birth: City, State, Country
  • Legal Guardian (if applicable): For minors

3.4 Present Address Details

Current Residential Address:

  • House No./Street: Complete address line 1
  • Village/Town/City: Current city of residence
  • District: Administrative district
  • State/UT: State or Union Territory
  • PIN Code: 6-digit postal code
  • Mobile Number: 10-digit active number
  • Email ID: Valid email address

3.5 Emergency Contact Information

Emergency Contact Person:

  • Name: Full name of emergency contact
  • Address: Complete address
  • Mobile Number: Active contact number
  • Email ID: Valid email address
  • Relationship: Father/Mother/Spouse/Sibling

3.6 References (2 Required)

Reference 1 & 2 Details:

  • Name: Full name of reference person
  • Address: Complete residential address
  • Phone Number: Contact number
  • Relationship: Known for how long
  • Occupation: Professional details

3.7 Previous Passport Details (If Applicable)

For Re-issue/Renewal Cases:

  • Passport Number: Previous passport number
  • Date of Issue: When it was issued
  • Place of Issue: Issuing authority
  • Date of Expiry: Expiration date
  • Reason for Re-issue: Expired/Lost/Damaged

3.8 Other Important Details

Additional Information:

  • Criminal Proceedings: Yes/No (if any pending cases)
  • Passport Refused: Yes/No (if ever refused before)
  • Passport Impounded: Yes/No (if ever impounded)
  • Identity Certificate: Yes/No (if ever held)
  • Other Nationality: Yes/No (dual citizenship)

3.9 Specific Field Instructions

Common Form Fields & How to Fill:

  • Applicant Name: Exactly as per birth certificate (no nicknames)
  • Aliases/Other Names: Include maiden names, previous names
  • Changed Name: Select 'Yes' if name changed after marriage/legally
  • Visible Identification Marks: Scars, moles, birthmarks (be specific)
  • Citizenship by Birth/Naturalization: Select 'Birth' for Indian-born
  • PAN Details: Enter PAN number if available (not mandatory)
  • Voter ID: Enter EPIC number if you have voter ID
  • Aadhaar Number: 12-digit Aadhaar number (mandatory)
  • Employment Details: Current employer name and address
  • Spouse Details: Required if married (name, nationality, passport details)

3.10 Address Section Details

Present Address vs Permanent Address:

  • Present Address: Where you currently live (must match address proof)
  • Permanent Address: Your permanent residence (can be same as present)
  • Is Present Address same as Permanent: Select appropriately
  • Police Station: Nearest police station to your present address
  • Years of Stay: How long you've lived at present address
  • Telephone Number: Landline number (if available, not mandatory)

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Tips for Form Filling:

  • Save Frequently: Click 'Save My Details' every 5 minutes
  • Exact Match: All names must match supporting documents exactly
  • No Abbreviations: Write full names, not short forms (e.g., 'Rajesh' not 'Raj')
  • Consistent Information: Same details across all sections
  • Valid Contacts: Ensure all phone numbers and emails are active
  • Address Proof: Present address should match address proof document
  • Date Format: Always use DD/MM/YYYY format
  • Capital Letters: Use proper case, not all CAPS

üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid:

  • Name Mismatch: Form name differs from birth certificate
  • Wrong Date Format: Using MM/DD/YYYY instead of DD/MM/YYYY
  • Incomplete Address: Missing PIN code or district details
  • Invalid References: Using family members as both references
  • Wrong Document Numbers: Incorrect Aadhaar or PAN numbers
  • Missing Spouse Details: Not filling spouse information if married
  • Inconsistent Employment: Different job titles in different sections
  • Invalid Email/Phone: Using inactive contact information

Step 4: Submit Application

  • Review all entered information carefully
  • Select 'I Agree' checkbox for terms and conditions
  • Click the 'Submit Form' button to submit the form
  • Pay application fee online

Step 5: View and Schedule Appointment

  • View your saved/submitted application
  • Schedule an appointment for visiting the PSK (Passport Seva Kendra)
  • Select your preferred date and time slot

Step 6: Passport Fees

  • Fresh Passport (36 pages): ‚Çπ1,500
  • Fresh Passport (60 pages): ‚Çπ2,000
  • Tatkal Service: Additional ‚Çπ2,000 (1-3 days processing)
  • Minor Passport: ‚Çπ1,000

Step 7: Print Application Form

After submitting your application:

  • Download the PDF application form from your account
  • Print on A4 size paper with good quality
  • Do not scale or resize while printing
  • Sign the form in the designated places
  • Paste a recent photograph (if required)

üìã Print Quality Tips:

  • Use laser printer for best results
  • Ensure all text is clearly visible
  • Check that barcodes are properly printed
  • Take multiple printouts as backup

Step 8: Required Documents Checklist

üìã Identity Proof (Any One)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • PAN Card
  • Driving License

üè† Address Proof (Any One)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Utility Bill (Electricity/Gas/Water)
  • Bank Statement (last 3 months)
  • Rent Agreement

üìÖ Date of Birth Proof (Any One)

  • Birth Certificate
  • 10th Class Marksheet/Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card

üìÑ Additional Documents

  • Printed Application Form (signed)
  • Appointment Confirmation
  • Fee Receipt (online payment)
  • Annexure A (if applicable for minors)
  • NOC from Employer (if government employee)
  • Marriage Certificate (if name changed after marriage)

üë∂ For Minor Applicants (Under 18)

  • Birth Certificate of minor
  • Aadhaar Card of minor (if available)
  • Passport copies of both parents
  • Annexure A (consent form signed by both parents)
  • Address proof of parents
  • In case of single parent: Court order/Death certificate

üíº For Government Employees

  • NOC (No Objection Certificate) from employer
  • Identity card issued by employer
  • Service certificate

Step 9: Visit PSK with Documents

Arrive 15 minutes before your appointment with all required documents. Complete biometric verification and document verification at the center.

PSK Visit Process:

  1. Security Check: No electronic items allowed
  2. Token Collection: Show appointment confirmation
  3. Document Verification: Submit originals + copies
  4. Biometric Collection: Fingerprints + photograph
  5. Final Review: Verify all details
  6. Acknowledgment: Collect receipt with tracking number

Step 10: Track Application Status

Normal processing takes 30-45 days, Tatkal service takes 1-3 days. Track your application status online using your file number.

Important Tips:

  • Save your details frequently while filling the form to avoid data loss
  • Ensure all documents are self-attested
  • Carry original documents for verification
  • Book Tatkal appointment for urgent travel
  • Double-check all information before submitting
  • Keep your application reference number safe for tracking
